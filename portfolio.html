Welcome to Alex Duncan's website. I play, make, and write about games.

Portfolio

Unless otherwise indicated, I worked as sole developer on the projects.

Every Creeping Thing

A 3D game where the player sees the world from the perspective of different animals. Sound design by Seth S. Scott. Find out more here.

The Soyuz Paradox

A puzzle game set on a doomed Soviet space station with a unique time travel mechanic. Collaboration with Alex Feigenbaum and José Tomás Vicuña. Find out more here.

Flora

Help a small tree survive the seasons and grow to be a giant of the forest. Play it here.

Metaphor

A game developed for the Metropolitan Museum of Art where players take pictures of art to match prompts. Collaboration with Caroline Porter and Inna Sheflyand. Check out our Instagram here.

Standoff

A minimalist, two-player duel game based on a scene from Kurosawa's Sanjuro. Work in progress.

Smoke Break

A short narrative game that is the internal monologue of a woman taking a smoke break. Work in progress.

Breakout

A game about popping pimples. Work in progress.

Porn or Art??

How fast can you tell if an image is Fine Art or a Playboy centerfold? Work in progress.

Composition

An arcade shooter style game inspired by the artwork of Piet Mondrian. Play it here.

Space Kitty

A digital and physical game where players use the flashlight from their phones to protect a space kitty from dogliens. Collaboration with Karina Popp.
